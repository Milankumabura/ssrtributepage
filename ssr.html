Sushant Singh Rajput

The most beloved actor of Bollywood

A tribute by- Milan K. Budha

Here's a time line of Sushant Singh Rajput's life:

  • 1986- Born in Patna,Bihar(India).
  • 2002- His family moved to Delhi following his mother's death in 2002 where Rajput completed his schooling for intermediate studies in Kulachi Hansraj Model School.
  • 2006- In 2006, he dropped out of his engineering course during his fourth year to begin a career in arts and performance.
  • 2008- Rajput was spotted by a casting team of Balaji Telefilms while he was working at the Prithvi Theatre.He was subsequently called for an audition and cast as a second lead in the television series Kis Desh Mein Hai Meraa Dil in 2008.
  • 2010- In Pavitra Rishta,he won an award for best actor in the Indian Television Academy Awards 2010.
  • 2013- He made his Bollywood debut in the film adaptation Kai Po Che! (2013) which became a commercial and critical success.
  • 2014- On 25 September 2014, Sushant Singh Rajput was announced as the actor who would play the role of Dhoni in the biopic which was titled M.S. Dhoni: The Untold Story.
  • 2017- won best actor for MS.Dhoni(The untold story) at Indian Film Festival Melbourne.
  • 2020- his last film "Dil bechara" was released posthumously.
  • 2020- On 14 June 2020, Rajput, aged 34, was found dead, hanging from the ceiling fan[1] in his home in Bandra, Mumbai.
